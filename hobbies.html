My Hobbies & Interests

I love getting lost in a good book. It's the perfect way for me to unwind, let my imagination run wild, and just see things from a totally different point of view.

Football is definitely my sport. I love hitting the pitch with my friends—it keeps me moving, but I also really enjoy the teamwork and the strategy behind the game. Nothing really beats the rush of playing a solid match.

Drawing

Whenever I need a creative break, I start drawing. I really enjoy sketching out my ideas and just playing around with different styles. It's super relaxing and helps me clear my head.

Playing Guitar

I picked up the guitar because I wanted to make my own music. It takes a lot of practice, but there’s an awesome feeling of satisfaction when you finally nail a song you've been working on for weeks.

Listening to Music

I pretty much always have music playing in the background. It helps me focus when I'm coding and just generally sets the vibe for my day. I listen to a bit of everything, depending on my mood.
